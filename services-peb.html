What We Offer

Core Services

Six specialised capabilities that cover every phase of your PEB project.

01 — Structural Systems

PEB Building Systems

LGSF Structures

Design & Engineering

Why PEB?

PEB vs Conventional Steel Building

Pre-Engineered Buildings consistently outperform conventional construction across every measurable dimension.

Parameter Pre-Engineered Building Conventional Building
Construction Time Usually Lower — faster erection Typically Higher
Material Cost Economical optimized steel usage Material variety leads to fluctuating costs
Labour Cost Usually Lower Typically Higher
Foundation Simple design — easy to construct Extensive heavy foundation required
Structure Weight 30% lighter on average Made up of heavy building components
Flexibility Future expansion easy and simple Future expansion is tedious and costly
Long-Term Maintenance Lower maintenance cost Varies based on materials used
Seismic Resistance Higher resistance to seismic forces Doesn't perform well in Seismic Zones
