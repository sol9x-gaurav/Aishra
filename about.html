Who We Are

Aishra Technofab Engineers

Established in August 2018, Aishra Technofab Engineers has rapidly grown into a trusted name in the field of LGSF and Pre-Engineered Buildings. We specialize in delivering quality products and turnkey solutions to a wide range of clients including Government / Semi-Government entities, Indian Railways, industries, warehouses, housing societies, fuel filling stations, and private sectors across India.

Our expertise extends beyond design, supply, and installation — we also manufacture high-quality PEB components in strict compliance with industry standards using state-of-the-art machinery.

  • CNC Plasma Cutting & CNC Plate Drilling Machines
  • PTW Beam Welding & Robotic Beam Stiffener Machines
  • Airless Shot Blasting Machines & Paint Spray Booth
  • Single-source solution: Design → Manufacture → Deliver → Install

Our Facility

State-of-the-Art Machinery

World-class equipment under one roof — enabling rapid, precise, and high-quality component production.

CNC Plasma Cutting

Hypertherm MAXPRO 200 — 3.0M width × 24.6M length, dross-free cutting on mild steel up to 20mm. Dual Oxyfuel + Plasma stations for all steel grades.

CNC Plate Drilling

1440 RPM spindle, drilling width 1.5M × length 3.0M. Plates up to 100mm thick — perfect bolt alignment for heavy-duty base plates and bridge connections.

Robotic Beam Stiffener

Automated Robotic Stiffener Welding — deep-penetration uniform welds exceeding international structural codes. Zero manual inconsistency.

PTW Beam Welding

Pull-Through Welding machine handles beams up to 1500mm flange width and 1420mm web height. Tapered frames with web taper up to 15°.

Airless Shot Blasting

SA 2.5 centrifugal shot blasting — removes all rust and mill scale for maximum coating adhesion, ensuring decades of corrosion resistance.

Paint Spray Booth

30,000 CFM Side Draft Booth applies Epoxy Primer + PU Paint in a controlled, dust-free environment — essential for industrial and coastal applications.

Our Production Power

Manufacturing Capabilities

Explore the technology and workflows that allow us to build India's strongest steel structures.

1. Extreme Scale & Versatility

  • Built-up beams width up to 1500 mm
  • Web heights up to 1420 mm
  • Plate thickness: webs 25 mm, flanges 40 mm
  • Tapered frames up to 15°
  • Single-piece spans up to 13 meters

2. CNC Precision-Drilled Plates & Flanges

  • Plate thickness up to 100 mm
  • 1440 RPM spindle for precision drilling
  • Drilling width: 1.5 m, length: 3.0 m
  • Drill size up to BT 40 (optional 50 mm)
  • Clean, burr-free holes & perfect bolt alignment

3. High-Definition CNC Plasma & Oxyfuel

  • Dual cutting: Plasma + Oxyfuel
  • Cutting width: 3.0 m, length: 24.6 m
  • Hypertherm MAXPRO 200 system
  • Dross-free cutting up to 20 mm (Pierce up to 32 mm)
  • Complex geometries and high-volume strip cutting

4. Web Splice Welded Products

  • Web widths: 200 mm to 1500 mm
  • Thickness: 4 mm to 25 mm
  • Full penetration welds
  • Maintains strength equivalent to parent metal
  • Ideal for long-span PEB structures

5. Robotic Welding & Structural Integrity

  • Pull-through welding
  • Robotic stiffener welding
  • Precision tack welding
  • Automated web-to-flange assembly
  • Uniform weld quality and perfect geometry

6. World-Class Surface Protection

  • SA 2.5 shot blasting standard
  • Epoxy primer + PU paint coating
  • Dust-free paint spray booth (30,000 CFM)
  • Long-lasting corrosion resistance

7. Rapid Project Turnaround

  • High-speed CNC processing
  • 6-torch strip cutting capability
  • Fully integrated workflow (cutting → drilling → welding → painting)
  • Single-source solution with reduced delays
