About the Product

Premium Purlin & Girt Profiles Manufacturing

High-tensile Z and C section purlins and girts form the critical structural secondary framing for premium heavy-duty roofs and walls. Roll-formed to exact specifications, they deliver outstanding spanning capabilities with reduced weight. These sections are pre-punched allowing swift, bolt-on execution in the field, optimizing the integration of primary frameworks with outer claddings seamlessly.
