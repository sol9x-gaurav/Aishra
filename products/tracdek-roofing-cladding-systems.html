TRACDEK Roofing &
Cladding Systems

About the Product

India's Leading Roofing System Since 1993

In 1993, Interarch introduced colour-coated engineered metal roof and wall systems in India. Since then Interarch has become a leading manufacturer and supplier of roofing and cladding systems used in major projects across the country.

TRACDEK systems deliver superior weather resistance, aesthetic versatility, and long-term performance — engineered specifically for India's diverse climate conditions.

System Range

Roof and Wall Profiles

Precision-engineered profiles for every application and aesthetic requirement.

Hi-Rib

High-rib standing seam profile offering superior strength and weatherproofing. Ideal for industrial roofing with large spans requiring minimal support structure.

Klippon

Concealed fix system providing a clean, modern appearance. The clip-based installation eliminates exposed fasteners, enhancing both aesthetics and waterproofing integrity.

SS-2000

Versatile structural standing seam profile combining high load-bearing capacity with weather-tightness. Suitable for both roofing and wall cladding applications.

Wall Profiles

Architectural wall cladding profiles available in various rib depths and widths. Provide structural integrity while delivering contemporary visual appeal for commercial and industrial facades.

Material Options

Material Finishes

Choose from a range of premium coated substrates engineered for durability and appearance.

Pre-Painted Galvalume Steel

Outstanding corrosion resistance combined with high-quality colour coating. Galvalume substrate provides 2–4× the corrosion life of standard galvanized steel.

Pre-Painted Galvanized Steel

Traditional zinc-coated steel with factory-applied colour coating. Cost-effective solution for standard industrial and commercial environments.

Pre-Painted Aluminum

Lightweight and naturally corrosion-resistant. Ideal for coastal environments and applications where weight reduction is critical.

Bare Galvalume Finish

Natural metallic finish offering self-healing properties. The bare Galvalume surface provides excellent protection without additional coating in many environments.

Proprietary Coating

TRACDEK Galvalume Plus

TRACDEK Galvalume Plus uses an advanced alloy coating that delivers exceptional corrosion resistance and significantly longer service life compared to conventional coatings.

  • ✦Superior corrosion resistance in aggressive environments
  • ✦2–4× longer service life vs standard galvanized
  • ✦Self-healing properties at cut edges and scratches
