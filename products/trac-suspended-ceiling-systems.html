About the Product

Engineered for Indian Building Conditions

The TRAC range of metal and glass reinforced gypsum ceilings is designed specifically for Indian building conditions and construction practices — delivering performance, aesthetics, and ease of installation in equal measure.

From expansive industrial spaces to refined commercial interiors, TRAC ceiling systems provide acoustic control, thermal management, and design versatility within a single, integrated system.

Product Range

Ceiling Systems

A comprehensive range of ceiling systems for interior and exterior applications.

84R Interior

Standard ribbed panel for interior commercial spaces with excellent acoustic performance.

84R Exterior

Weather-resistant ribbed panel engineered for external soffits and canopy applications.

84C / 184C Interior

Corrugated profile offering visual depth and superior structural rigidity for large spans.

84C Exterior

Exterior-grade corrugated panel with enhanced weather sealing for exposed applications.

150F Interior / Exterior

Flat panel system delivering a clean, contemporary aesthetic. Available for both interior and exterior use.

150C Interior

Wide corrugated interior panel providing bold architectural expression in large spaces.

Tile Lay-In Systems

Accessible tile systems allowing easy service access while maintaining a uniform ceiling plane.

Clip-In Ceiling Systems

Premium concealed fixing system providing a seamless, jointless ceiling appearance.
